Biography

Hari Bansha Acharya is one of the most famous Nepali celebrity of Nepal/ He is a well renowned public figure. He is an actor, a comedian, a public activist, singer and song writer.Hari Bansha Acharya was born on 27th Kartik 2014 BS 13 November 1958 in Gairidhara, Kathmandu. Hari Bansha Acharya was married to Meera Acharya 2040 BS. They had two sons together. His wife, who was suffering from a heart disease for a long time, sadly passed away due to heart attack in April 20, 2011. This left the great comedian in great shock and terror. But he later got remarried to Ramila Pathak (who also had a son with her late first husband) after one and a half years of his first wife's demise.Hari Bansha Acharya has worked in many films, including: Lovipapi, Filim, Rajamati, Silu, Balidaan, Je Bho Ramrai bho, and Tan ta sarhai bigrish ni badri, which are some of his most popular films. His theatre programs include Yamlok, Paralysis, Aina, Cockfight and many more. He has also been a part of the Gaijatra programs for decades.
