Hari Bansha Acharya

Biography

Hari Bansha Acharya was born on 13th November 1957 in Gaidhara. In addition to being a musician and novelist, he is well-known for his humorous role as an actor. Together with his irreplaceable partner, Madan Krishna Shrestha, Acharya has made outstanding contributions to the Nepali entertainment industry. Every Nepalese person's heart was captured by the Maha Jodi.Beginning at an early age, Hari Bansha Acharya battled to make ends meet throughout his life. Acharya, with less schooling but with greater wisdom, has inspired thousands of people via his books and won their hearts by performing.

Bansha Hari Acharya was raised in a traditional household where his parents encouraged him to pursue studies in Rudri and Sanskrit. They desired for him to grow up to be a well-known Pandit. He was therefore prohibited from attending school like other regular children.Acharya was a cheeky child who was not at all submissive. His disposition was inherently humorous, and he would amuse his pals by making comical impersonations of their professors. Without Acharya, the friends used to remark, it would be lonely.
